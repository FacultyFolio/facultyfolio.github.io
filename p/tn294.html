Research interestsI am mostly interested in the theoretical and algorithmic aspects of machine learning motivated by real-world problem settings. I like to seek a mathematical understanding of the underlying algorithmic principles for learning with strong adaptivity to problem structures, and thereby design efficient machine learning algorithms with strong theoretical guarantees. The current topics include, but not limited to, reinforcement learning (e.g., Offline RL, Multi-agent, Multi-task), theory of reasoning with transformers, and responsible AI (e.g., privacy, robustness, unlearning). I direct the Statistical and Algorithmic ML Lab @ NJIT.
