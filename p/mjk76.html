Teaching interestsI Teach Classes On How To Build Better Software. At The Undergrad Level, That Means The Tools And Techniques For Being A Good Software Developer. At The Graduate Level, That Usually Means The Tools And Techniques For Doing Verification And Building Effective Verification Tools. You Can Think Of My Grad Classes As Being One Meta-Level Higher Than The Undergrad Classes: My Undergrad Classes Cover How To Be A Good Developer Yourself; My Grad Classes Mostly Cover How To Build Tools That Make Others (And You) Better Developers.
